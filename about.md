## What is DevCur?

**DevCur** (Developmental Curiosity) is the research blog of the [Flowers Lab](https://flowers.inria.fr) (Inria, Univ. Bordeaux). We explore developmental AI, curiosity-driven learning, and embodied intelligence, targeting a wide and interdisciplinary audience.

## Key Research Themes

**Human autotelic learning**
We use methods from psychology to design new experimental protocols, and computational theories to test hypotheses about human curiosity, metacognition, language, and social interaction.

**Machine autotelic learning**
We design open-ended learning machines that are autotelic, data-frugal, with strong generalisation skills, leveraging language and social interaction to integrate within human cultures. We improve frugality and grounding of generative AI systems using autotelic deep reinforcement learning.

**Educational technologies & Assisted scientific discovery**
We build tools that stimulate curiosity-driven learning, meta-cognition, and creativity in humans across the lifespan. We also study how autotelic exploration algorithms can help scientists make discoveries in complex systems.
